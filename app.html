Simple Rules
  • Never mix glycolic acid and niacinamide in the same evening.
  • Always wait 3–10 minutes between active layers to avoid irritation.
  • Sunscreen every morning — even on cloudy days or near windows.
  • Patch test new products on inner arm for 24 hours before face use.
  • Moisturize immediately after cleansing while skin is still slightly damp.
  • Stay hydrated — good skin starts from within. 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Reapply sunscreen every 2 hours when outdoors in direct sun.
  • Reduce exfoliation if skin feels tight or red — give it recovery days.

🌐 How to Choose Skincare Products
🥤 Oily: Gel cleanser • Niacinamide 10% • BHA toner 3x/wk • Water-gel moisturiser • SPF 50+ matte
❄️ Dry: Cream cleanser • Ceramide moisturiser • Hyaluronic acid serum • Facial oil • Moisturising SPF
♾️ Combo: Gentle cleanser • Lightweight moisturiser • BHA T-zone only • SPF 50+
🌸 Sensitive: Fragrance-free only • Cetaphil/CeraVe • No actives until stable • Mineral SPF

🌌 Nepal Skincare Tips
☀️ Monsoon Jun–Sep: Gel moisturiser + BHA 3x/week + SPF daily. Reapply SPF every 2 hrs outdoors.
❄️ Winter Dec–Feb: Ceramide cream + facial oil. Humidity drops to 30% — barrier repair is priority.
⚠️ UV Alert: Kathmandu UV index 10–12 (EXTREME) Mar–Oct. SPF 50+ PA++++ every morning without exception.
💧 Hydration: Apply moisturiser within 3 minutes of washing — on damp skin for best absorption.
💊 Acne: Niacinamide 10% morning + evening, BHA toner 3x/week evening = most effective Nepal acne combo.
